# MedAssistAI: Smart Healthcare Ecosystem

---

## 🚨 The Problem

In emergency situations, **blind routing** is a critical failure point. Ambulances and first responders are often dispatched to the nearest hospital — regardless of whether that facility has the capacity, specialists, or equipment needed for the patient's condition. This results in:

- Delayed treatment due to hospital overcrowding
- Mismatched care (wrong specialty, unavailable resources)
- Higher mortality rates in time-critical emergencies

---

## 💡 The Solution

**MedAssistAI** replaces guesswork with **AI-driven triage and intelligent hospital routing**. By analyzing patient vitals, severity levels, GPS location, and real-time hospital capacity, the system ranks and assigns the optimal care facility — in seconds.

The result: the right patient reaches the right hospital at the right time.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **XGBoost Hospital Ranking** — A machine learning model scores and ranks nearby hospitals based on patient condition, severity, and facility capabilities, ensuring the best possible match.
- **Real-Time WebSockets** — Live, bidirectional communication keeps dispatchers, responders, and hospitals synchronized throughout the emergency lifecycle with zero polling overhead.
- **Encrypted Medical IDs** — Patient identity and medical history are protected with end-to-end encryption, ensuring privacy compliance and secure data access during triage.
